Gaming Genres

What kind of games are there?

Now you're interested in gaming, but you dont really know what kind of games you want to play. Well here you can read about the most popular gaming genres out there. So dive in and see which ones suits and interests you!

SHOOTER

How do action games sound to ya? 'Cause they sure are fun to us. You love shooting stuff down? well we got the perfect genre for you.

Shooter games are technically a subgenre of action games, but with how popular they are, they are basically their own genre now. As the name implies, in these games the player will control a character and use a weapon usually a guns or projectile based weapons which can be used in combination with other tools such as grenades for indirect offense, armor for additional defense, or accessories such as telescopic sights to modify the behavior of the weapons. A common resource found in many shooter games is ammunition, armor or health, or upgrades which augment the player character's weapons.

Looking to shoot something?

ACTION

Action packed game that thrills any good player. The player is in control and is challenged in things such as hand-eye coordination and quick reflexes. Keep an eye out!

In an action game, the player typically controls a character often in the form of a protagonist or avatar. This player character must navigate a level, collecting objects, avoiding obstacles, and battling enemies with their natural skills as well as weapons and other tools at their disposal. At the end of a level or group of levels, the player must often defeat a boss enemy that is more challenging and often a major antagonist in the game's story. Enemy attacks and obstacles deplete the player character's health and lives, and the player receives a game over when they run out of lives.

Alternatively, the player gets to the end of the game by finishing a sequence of levels to complete a final goal, and see the credits. But some action games, such as early arcade games, are unbeatable and have an indefinite number of levels; with the player's only goal being to get as far as they can to maximize their score.

Looking for some action?

STRATEGY

These games do not require godlike reflexes or reaction times, but they do require slight intelligence. So if you want to play something that gets your senses and mind checked, this, my friend, is the genre for you.

Strategy video games are a genre of video game that emphasize skillful thinking and planning to achieve victory. Specifically, a player must plan a series of actions against one or more opponents, and the reduction of enemy forces is usually a goal. Victory is achieved through superior planning, and the element of chance takes a smaller role. In most strategy video games, the player is given a godlike view of the game world, and indirectly controls game units under their command. Thus, most strategy games involve elements of warfare to varying degrees, and feature a combination of tactical and strategic considerations. In addition to combat, these games often challenge the player's ability to explore, or manage an economy.

Looking to use that big brain of yours?

HORROR

Looking for a scare and not sleep in 4 days?? Do you always complain that characters from horror movie do predictable things? Well now you can experience absolute terror yourself and see how you deal with it! Good luck, lol.

Horror games are usually combined with other genres such as survival or action games, but it always share a common theme, they are made to scare you. So unlike other video games that are classified by their gameplay, horror games are nearly always based on narrative or visual presentation, and use a variety of gameplay types.

Looking for a good scare?

SURVIVAL

You're an adventurist who loves living of the wild, you want to test your survival skills, so you know if you're ready for the sure to come apocalypse? Do you want to become Frank de la Jungla? This is your type of game.

Survival games are considered an extension of a common video game theme where the player character is stranded or separated from others and must work alone to survive and complete a goal. Survival games focus on the survival parts of these games, while encouraging exploration of an open world. They are primarily action games, though some gameplay elements present in the action-adventure genre‚Äîsuch as resource management and item crafting‚Äîare commonly found in survival games, and are central elements in titles like Survival Kids. At the start of a typical survival game, the player is placed alone in the game's world with few resources. It is not uncommon for players to spend the majority or entirety of the game without encountering a friendly non-player character; since NPCs are typically hostile to the player, the emphasis is placed on avoidance, rather than confrontation. In some games, however, combat is unavoidable and provides the player with valuable resources (e.g., food, weapons, and armor).

Looking to survive?

RACING

Get your hands on the wheel, we are going racing!! Get to the finish line first and enjoy the champagne!

Racing video games are a video game genre in which the player participates in a racing competition. They may be based on anything from real-world racing leagues to fantastical settings. They are distributed along a spectrum between simulations with very realistic design and simplified arcade-style racing games.
